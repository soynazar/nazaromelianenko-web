How I work

I work with respect for established processes, responsibility in every decision, and direct communication. I value structure, consistency, and high-quality work as the foundation for any meaningful progress.

Education

I teach Spanish and Valencian to adults, with a specialized focus on learners coming from diverse linguistic and cultural backgrounds.

I integrate Social-Emotional Learning (SEL) strategies and foster a positive learning environment: safety, clarity, and a sustainable pace. I pay special attention to preparing for official certifications like DELE and EOI, embedding test prep within a solid learning journey.

Technology

I develop web projects focused on real utility, user experience (UX), and clean code that is easy to understand and evolve.

I am currently pursuing professional training in Application Development (DAM) while building personal projects to refine my technical criteria and practice.

Communication

Beyond code and grammar, I specialize in the effective transmission of ideas. I have over 6 years of experience creating professional audiovisual content, presenting, and moderating live broadcasts in both Russian and Spanish.

This facet allows me to add value to projects requiring public visibility, team training, or the creation of corporate materials.
