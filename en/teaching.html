What I teach

I teach Spanish and Valencian, with a specialized approach for students coming from different linguistic and cultural backgrounds.

The classes are suitable both for beginners and for those who want to refine their fluency and confidence when speaking.

Methodology

My method combines structure, practical application, and guidance based on a Positive Learning Environmentand principles of Emotional Intelligence (SEL).

This means learning without stress but with real demands, overcoming barriers and maintaining constant motivation.

Exam preparation

I specialize in preparation for official certifications such as DELE and EOI, integrating exam study into the real language learning process.

Format

Online classes with personalized support materials. I work with clear objectives, regular progress tracking, and a realistic study plan.
