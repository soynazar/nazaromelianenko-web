Teaching · Languages

I work with people who seek serious learning, adapted to their pace and with close attention to the learning process.

What I teach

I teach Spanish and Valencian, with a specialized approach for students coming from different linguistic and cultural backgrounds.

Classes are suitable both for beginners and for those who want to refine their fluency and confidence when speaking.

Methodology

My method combines structure, practical application, and close guidance, based on a Positive Learning Environment and principles of Social and Emotional Learning (SEL).

This means learning without stress but with real expectations, overcoming barriers while maintaining consistent motivation.

Exam preparation

I specialize in preparing students for official certifications such as DELE and EOI, integrating exam preparation into the real process of language acquisition.

Format

Online classes with personalized support materials. I work with clear objectives, regular progress tracking, and a realistic study plan.
