Teaching · Languages

Subjects

I teach Spanish and Valencian, with a specialized focus on learners coming from different linguistic and cultural backgrounds.

Methodology

My method combines structure, practical application, and a supportive environment through a Positive Learning Environment and Social-Emotional Learning (SEL).

This means learning in a stress-free but high-standard atmosphere, overcoming barriers while maintaining steady motivation.

Exam Preparation

I specialize in preparing for official certifications such as DELE and EOI, integrating exam training into the actual language learning process.

Format

Online lessons with personalized support materials. I work by goals, with regular progress tracking and a realistic study plan.
